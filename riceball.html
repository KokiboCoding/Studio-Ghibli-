Onigiri - Spirited Away


Ingredients

    - 2 cups of rice
    - 4 cups of water (for rice cooker!)
    - 1 or 2 cup of warm water
    - Salt

Directions

    - Place the rice in rice pot - wash the rice until the water becomes no longer murky and clear. When measuring the water. *Tip* have your fingertip touch the rice and the water should reach the first line on your finger.
    - Put the bowl in the rice cooker and have it do it's magic!
    - After the rice is cooked, let it cool it a bit as it would be too hot to the touch.
    - Place salt in a bowl of warm water and mix it.
    - Once the rice is cool, wet hands with the salt water and make a triangle or circular shape

Then BAM! You got your onigiri! (PS: you can add Japanese seasoning or seaweed)
