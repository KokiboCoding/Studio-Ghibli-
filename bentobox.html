Bento Box - My Neighbor Totoro


Ingredients

    - 1 cup of cooked rice!
    - Shishamo
    - Umeboshi
    - Edamame
    - Sakura Denbu
    - 1 tbsp oil

Directions

    - Put cooked rice in the bento box about 3/4 to leave some room.
    - Take the 1 umeboshi and put it on top of the rice.
    - In a pan, use the 1 tbsp oil and cook one shishamo on both sides until cooked before placing it into the bento box.
    - Add your salted cooked edamame on top of the shishamo.
    - Then sprinkle the sakura denbu on to the rice.

Enjoy your bento box!
